About

Target

Urban, ecofriendly and local-consuming people who need to manage their reusable containers with their local retailers and who want to reduce their plastic waste and environmental footprint.

Pain

Limiting waste and anticipating AGEC regulations on the end of single-use plastics.

Solution

An easy-to-use application for retailers & consummers

A solution to manage reusable containers for retailers (restaurants, caterers, bakeries...) and consumers.

Retailers: they have access to their containers stock at a glance.

Consummers: they can find all the partner retailers and order directly via the application and thanks to the dashboard, they can follow their container rentals in real time.
